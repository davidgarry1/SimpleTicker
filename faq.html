FAQ

Why is Bitcoin Cash (BCH) not listed?

GDAX recently added Bitcoin Cash (BCH), not to be confused with Bitcoin (BTC), and we will including it shortly. If you would like to know more about the differences between BTC and BTH, you can read more on Slate.

How real-time is the data?

The last trade price of a currency is updated every second, and therefore is 500ms delayed on average. The charts are updated every 60 seconds.

Where does the data come from?

The live price information comes from GDAX, which is the underlying cryptocurrency exchange of the Coinbase app. We are not affiliated with GDAX nor Coinbase. The historic chart data comes from the Cryptocompare API.

Why do some currencies show BTC price instead of GBP?
